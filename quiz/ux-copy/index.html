UX / Copywriting

Before you begin

UX writing is interface design with words: buttons, errors, empty states, onboarding, and confirmations. Strong copy reduces support burden, builds trust, and reflects inclusion. You’ll see prompts about plain language, consistency, CTAs, and recovery patterns. Apply what you learn to your style guide and critique real screens.

You will answer 10 multiple-choice questions. Each has four options, one correct and three incorrect. After each question, rate how confident you felt.
